## 📌 Overview

Cairo PC Health Scanner provides clear and detailed information about your
computer's health and performance. The program monitors system performance,
startup programs, disk health, background services, and storage usage —
delivering a complete assessment without requiring technical knowledge.
It operates in read-only mode, making no changes to your system, with all
data stored locally on your device.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ Comprehensive system performance and resource monitoring
- ✅ Scan startup programs and evaluate their impact
- ✅ Complete disk health analysis
- ✅ Scan background services and diagnose performance issues
- ✅ Detailed storage usage statistics
- ✅ Clear and easy-to-understand overall system health score
- ✅ Generate and export detailed system health reports
- ✅ Privacy-focused read-only scanning — no system changes
- ✅ Administrator access support when required
- ✅ Real-time status updates during scanning
- ✅ Lightweight and does not affect system stability
- ✅ 100% Free — no fees or subscriptions

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Download and Install** the software on your device
2. **Open the software** — the main screen shows your system health status
3. **Click Start Scan** to perform a full system scan
4. **Monitor Progress** — stop the scan anytime if needed
5. **Review Results** — view detailed health and performance results
6. **Check Each Item** — review details and improvement recommendations
7. **Export Report** — save a detailed health report for later review
